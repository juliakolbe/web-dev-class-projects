About

Hi, I'm Julia. I was born and raised in Durban, South Africa. I grew up playing hockey, reading Shakespeare, and watching fantastic films with my family. I have always loved art and design, and remember creating my first website at aged 11 (although, to be fair, it was a basic Wix template). Later on, I obtained a liberal arts degree in English Literature, History, and Philosophy from the University of Pretoria, South Africa.

I came to the US in the Fall of 2023 to study Computer Science at Franciscan University of Steubenville. I've had a tough time learning to code, but I've been able to push through after many hard hours of work, as well as help from kind classmates and professors. This summer, I'll be interning for a start-up company in Virginia, and I could not be more thrilled. It's my primary motivation for my doing well this semester.

One day, I want to make a website for my mom, Bridget, and her business.
